About


Why

In the current era, a human face is not only used for social interactions but also extensively utilized to safety precautions and to monitor various covid protocol like face mask detection. This particular technology is well known as ACP stands for AI system for covid prevention protocol. It utilizes the human face to detect the mask and measure temperature. As technology advances, this system is getting more potential in various industries ranging from Enterprises, Hospitals, Law enforcement, Retail, Hospitality to public places.


Proposal

ACP is the future generation mask detection system that provides an incredibly versatile human mask detection and temperature measurement process. Its application is not restricted for the security concerns only but also promptly expands the outreach in a commercial domain.

Our system ACP captures still images or live video images to identify an individual through a certain distance as it does not involve any physical interaction with the person. The images captured will be further validated for mask and temperature measurement.

The non-contact procedure rendered by our technology provides a safe, rapid and reliable solution as compared to other systems like a physical temperature measurement with thermometer, mask validation by human etc.
